Countries Of The World Quiz

How To Play

Are you ready to test your mastery of the countries of the world?

Use the dropdown menus to toggle quiz mode and the region you want to be quizzed on.

Drag to pan the map and pinch or scroll to zoom.

Mode 1 - Name All Countries

Can you name every country? Countries will show up in color once found. Try to color the whole map!

Mode 2 - Find The Country

Test your ability to find countries on a map. The control panel will indicate a country. Locate and click on the country to win.

Mode 3 - Name The Country

The map will zoom to and highlight a country. Can you name it?
