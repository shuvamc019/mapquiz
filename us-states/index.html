US States Quiz

How To Play

Are you ready to test your mastery of the US states?

Use the dropdown menus to toggle the game mode you want to play. Pinch or scroll to zoom the map and drag to pan.

Mode 1 - Name All States

Can you name every state? Initially, the map will appear completely gray. States will show up in color as you name them. Try to color the whole map!

Mode 2 - Find The State

Test your knowledge of where states are located. The control panel will indicate a state name. It's your job to find that state on the map and click on it.

Mode 3 - Name The State

Challenge how well you can identify states just from their appearance. The map will zoom to and highlight a state and it's up to you to name it.
